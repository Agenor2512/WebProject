Spicy vegetal burger with sesame salad

  • 1/2 cup green lentils (cooked first)
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 buns

    For the sesame salad :

  • The juice of a squeezed lemon
  • 2 tablespoons sesame cream (tahini)
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 2 cups grated red cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to season !

Step 1

Pour a cup of lentil into a large bowl and mash well.
Stir in the remaining whole lentils.
Take your grated carrots and time them with a paper towel
to remove excess moisture.

Step 2

Take your tomato sauce and season it with pepper.
Then incorporate all the ingredients until you get a good consistency.
If it looks too wet, you can add 1 to 2 tablespoons of flour.
Divide and shape the mixture into 4 patties.

Step 3

Put the oil in a pan and fry the patties until brown.
3 to 4 minutes for each side.

Step 4

Now you have to prepare the sesame salad.
Put the juice lemon, sesame cream and sugar in a bowl.
Mix well. Add the cabbage, carrots and parsley.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5

Put it all in the buns. Enjoy !
