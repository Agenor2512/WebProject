Leek goat cheese quiche for 4 people

  • 3 leeks
  • 20 cl fresh cream
  • 10 cl milk
  • black pepper
  • Fine salt
  • 1 shortcut pastry
  • 1 goat cheese
  • 3 eggs

Step 1

Spread the dough in the buttered and floured mould.

Step 2

Clean and slice the whites of the 3 leeks. Cut them.

Step 4

Spread the leeks on the pie base.

Step 5

Preheat the oven to 180°C (thermostat 6).

Step 6

In a bowl, beat the eggs, cream and milk.
Season to taste and pour the mixture into the pie.

Step 7

Cut the goat cheese into slices and place them on the quiche.

Step 8

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. And enjoy!
