Zucchini egg for 4 persons

  • 8 eggs
  • 400 g zucchini
  • 100 g Paris mushrooms
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes
  • 40 g grated Emmental cheese
  • 20 g olive oil
  • 10 g parsley

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 200Â°C.

Step 2

Cut your 400 gr of zucchini into thin slices.
Chop the mushrooms and cut the cherry tomatoes.
Mix with the olive oil!

Step 3

Take cassolettes and break two eggs in each.
Then put them to cook in a double boiler for 10 to 15 minutes.

Step 4

Brown your parsley and spread it over the prepared parts. Enjoy!
