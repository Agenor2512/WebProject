Single leek quiche for 4 people.

  • 1 shortcut pastry
  • 2 leeks
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 cl of liquid cream
  • 1 pinch nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 30 g Emmental

Step 1

Peel the leeks, rinse them under fresh water, then slice them.
Heat the oil in a frying pan.
Add the leeks and cook for 10 min, stirring often.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 180°C.
Spread the dough, line a mould pie (spread with butter)
and prick the bottom with a fork.

Step 3

In a bowl, whisk the eggs with the liquid cream.
Add the grated Emmental and nutmeg. Salt and pepper everything.

Etape 4

Spread the leeks over the dough and cover with the egg/cream mixture. Bake for 35 min. Serve hot and enjoy!
